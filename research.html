Research

Research Objective

To apply Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques for solving real-world problems in education, healthcare, and societal development.

Research Projects

Adapting LLM for Regional Language Processing

Objective: To adapt Large Language Models (LLMs) through fine-tuning on task-specific and language-specific datasets, enhancing their ability to handle cultural context, linguistic diversity, and domain-specific nuances of the Marathi language.

Outcome: Developed three specialized models—Marathi_Para, Marathi_Class, and Marathi_Senti—which significantly outperformed the base model in both quantitative metrics (e.g., 90% accuracy for classification and 70% for sentiment) and qualitative measures such as fluency, relevance, and correctness.

Adapting LLM for Question Paper Generation

Objective: To utilize Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to integrate LLMs with external academic course materials, ensuring that generated question papers are contextually relevant, syllabus-aligned, and pedagogically sound according to Bloom’s Taxonomy.

Outcome: Developed an AI-based system that demonstrated higher alignment with course objectives and improved syllabus coverage compared to generic models, producing structured, diverse, and ready-to-use examination papers.

Adapting LLM for Student Assessment Evaluation

Objective: To adapt LLM embeddings (specifically OpenAI ADA-002) and cosine similarity to create semantic representations of student answers and compare them with model answers for fair, transparent, and scalable automated grading.

Outcome: Developed a system that evaluates descriptive answers with high accuracy, closely matching subject-expert grading with low Root Mean Square Error (RMSE), while reducing bias and human error in the evaluation process.

Adapting LLM for Student Support System

Objective: To enhance LLM capabilities (using Gemini 1.5 Pro) by integrating Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with a university-specific knowledge base to provide precise, context-aware, and multilingual administrative support.

Outcome: Developed an advanced chatbot that outperformed generic models in accuracy and relevance, significantly reducing hallucinations by retrieving information directly from authenticated university documents.
